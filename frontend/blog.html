Understanding Mental Health

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act in everyday life. It influences how we handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions.

Good mental health isn’t just the absence of mental illness. It also includes emotional balance, resilience, and the ability to cope with life’s challenges while maintaining a sense of purpose and well-being.

Managing Stress in Daily Life

Stress is a natural part of life, but when it becomes overwhelming, it can affect both mental and physical well-being.

Managing stress effectively starts with small, consistent habits. Practices like mindfulness, regular exercise, quality sleep, and setting healthy boundaries can significantly reduce stress.

The Importance of Self-Care

Self-care is the practice of intentionally taking care of your mental, emotional, and physical well-being.

It includes healthy routines, mindfulness, rest, and emotional boundaries. Regular self-care helps prevent burnout, improves resilience, and supports long-term mental health.
